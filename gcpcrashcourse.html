Overview


What is #GCPCrashCourse?

#GCPCrashCourse is a Cloud based learning campaign that focuses on building Learning Path's for students. With #GCPCrashCourse you get to learn dedicated topics in Application Development, Data or Machine Learning. We will be conducting a free month-long online GCP(Google Cloud Platform) Crash Course courses on Qwiklabs that you can complete at ease of your home, with DSC Core Team acting as instructors and helping you in the journey.


Why you should take the course?

It is a Project-oriented certified course which will add feathers to your skills cart. It is bound to be a learning and fun experience. The entire course is free and open to all. The course can be easily grasped by someone with minimum prior knowledge and hence no pre-requisites needed. In case someone finds difficulty, contact us. This course has been curated by the Google Cloud Team and would be available only through the month of October 2019. On completion of any one of the three tracks, you would receive badges on your profile and these can be directly shared on LinkedIn or similar platforms.

This course is extremely beneficial for students aspiring to take these tracks as career as the course is made for universities keeping the industry demands and standards in mind. Hence, every student is expected to leverage this opportunity to the fullest.

Only things that you require to complete this campaign are a Laptop and an active internet connection and you are good to go. Every quest comes with detailed step wise instructions, just follow those steps and you should have no problem completing this quests.
